Privacy Policy for Valley Water Mobile
Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Valley Water ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Valley Water Mobile application. This policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

A. Location Data
To provide accurate logistics and delivery services, we collect your location data.

Background Location: Since this is a logistics app, we collect location data in the background even when the app is closed or not in use. This information is used to:
Track delivery progress and provide ETAs to customers.
Verify that customer visits were performed at the correct site.
Optimize dispatching and routing for the team.
User Control: You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings, but disabling it will significantly limit the app's functionality (e.g., you may not be able to record deliveries or visits).
B. Camera and Gallery Access
We require access to your device’s camera and photo gallery to:

Proof of Delivery: Allow you to take and upload photos of delivered shipments.
QR Scanning: Use the scanner to identify assets, products, or tasks.
C. Personal and Authentication Data
Identification: We collect login credentials (usernames/passwords) to secure your account.
Signatures: We collect digital signatures as part of the "Proof of Delivery" process.
D. Device and Usage Data
We collect information about your device (device_info_plus) and how you interact with our app (via Firebase Crashlytics and Remote Config) to ensure stability and fix technical issues.

2. Third-Party Services
We use third-party service providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service:

Firebase (Google): For push notifications (FCM), crash reporting (Crashlytics), and configuration management.
Google Maps: To provide navigation and mapping services. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.
3. Data Retention and Security
We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our logistics services and comply with business records requirements. We use industry-standard security measures (SSL/TLS encryption via our API) to protect your data during transmission and storage.

4. Disclosure of Data
We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only:

With authorized company personnel for operational management.
If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
